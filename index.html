FAQ

General Pricing Process Results
What exactly does Aether do?−

We help ecommerce brands find what is blocking profitable growth, then fix the system behind it: data, tracking, GTM, paid ads, SEO, AI search, CRO, retention, and growth modeling.

We are not here to just run campaigns. We help you understand what is broken, what to fix first, and how to scale with more clarity.

What kinds of brands do you work with?+

We work with ecommerce and DTC brands that already have traction but feel growth is getting harder, more expensive, or less predictable.

Most clients come to us when they are spending on acquisition, getting traffic, but still asking: "Why is profit not scaling the way revenue is?"

How is Aether different from a performance agency?+

Most performance agencies start with campaigns. We start with the gap.

Before we ask you to spend more, we look at what may be blocking growth: messy tracking, weak positioning, unclear offer, poor landing pages, low retention, slow SEO, or broken data.

Then we choose the right fix.

How quickly do you see results?+

You usually see clarity first. The first win is knowing what is actually blocking growth.

After that, speed depends on the gap. Tracking and CRO fixes can show early movement quickly. SEO, retention, and growth modeling compound over time.

We do not promise overnight growth. We build the system that makes growth easier to trust.

How do you price your work?−

Pricing depends on the size of the problem, the services needed, and whether we are diagnosing, building, managing, or scaling the full growth system.

We usually start with a growth audit so we can understand the gap before recommending a scope.

Do I need a full retainer?+

Not always.

Some brands need a focused audit. Some need help fixing tracking or CRO. Others need full growth support across GTM, ads, SEO, AI search, retention, and data modeling.

We recommend the smallest scope that can create the clearest next move.

What if we already have an agency?+

That is common.

We can work alongside your existing agency or internal team. Our role is to find the gaps, clarify the growth system, and help everyone focus on the highest-leverage fixes.

Sometimes we replace an agency. Sometimes we make the current setup work better.

Is this only for brands with big budgets?+

No, but you need enough traction for the data to mean something.

If you are already spending on growth and have traffic, orders, and customer data, we can usually find useful gaps. If you are too early, we may recommend a lighter strategy or audit first.

What happens first?−

We start with diagnosis.

We look at your data, tracking, funnel, GTM, ads, SEO, CRO, retention, and growth numbers to find where the system is unclear or leaking.

Then we show what to fix first.

What do you need from us?+

Usually access to the main growth data sources: Shopify or ecommerce platform, GA4, ad accounts, search data, email platform, analytics, and any reporting you currently use.

We also ask for context: what you have tried, what worked, what failed, and where growth feels stuck.

Do you start running ads immediately?+

No. That is the point.

If the gap is tracking, offer, landing page, CRO, SEO, or retention, launching more ads first can make the problem more expensive.

We run ads when the system is ready to learn from them.

What do you fix first?+

The highest-leverage gap.

That could be tracking, positioning, offer, landing page, product page, checkout, paid ads, SEO, AI visibility, retention, or growth modeling.

We do not force every brand into the same service plan.

What results should we expect?−

You should expect clearer growth decisions, better measurement, sharper GTM, stronger acquisition, better conversion paths, and a more reliable system for scaling.

The exact result depends on where the gap is. The goal is simple: help the same growth effort produce better business outcomes.

How do you measure success?+

We look beyond surface-level ROAS.

Depending on the work, we may track contribution margin, CAC, LTV, conversion rate, revenue by channel, repeat purchase, checkout drop-off, organic visibility, forecast accuracy, and profit efficiency.

The metric should match the gap.

What if ads are already working?+

Great. Then we check what is happening around them.

Sometimes ads are working, but the site is leaking. Sometimes CRO is good, but retention is weak. Sometimes revenue is growing, but margin is not.

Working ads do not always mean the growth system is healthy.

What if the problem is not ads?+

Then we do not sell you ads.

If the biggest gap is tracking, GTM, SEO, CRO, retention, or data modeling, we focus there first.

That is why we diagnose before prescribing.
