01Overview

Ather LLC ("Ather", "we", "us") is an ecommerce growth studio. This policy explains what personal information we collect when you visit our website, book a call, subscribe to our newsletter, or engage us for services — and what we do with it. By using our site or services, you agree to the practices described here.

02Information we collect

We only collect what we need to operate, improve, and deliver our services:

  • Information you give us: your name, email, company, revenue range, and the details you share in our contact form, on a call, or during an engagement.
  • Information collected automatically: device and browser type, pages viewed, referring URLs, and approximate location, gathered through cookies and similar technologies.
  • Engagement data: where you grant access, analytics, ad-platform, and store data needed to perform an audit or retainer. We access this under the permissions you provide and never sell it.

03How we use information

We use the information we collect to respond to enquiries and schedule calls, to perform and improve our services, to send updates you've subscribed to, to maintain the security of our systems, and to comply with our legal obligations. We do not use client store data for any purpose beyond the engagement you've agreed to.

04Cookies & tracking

Our site uses essential cookies to function and optional analytics cookies to understand how the site is used. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling some may affect site functionality. We honour "Do Not Track" signals where technically feasible.

05Sharing & disclosure

We never sell your personal information. We share it only with trusted service providers who help us operate (for example, hosting, email, and analytics providers), each bound by confidentiality and data-protection obligations, or when required by law, or as part of a business transfer in which this policy continues to apply.

06Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes described here, typically the duration of our relationship plus any period required for legal, accounting, or reporting obligations. Engagement data is returned or deleted on request at the end of a project, subject to applicable law.

07Your rights

Depending on where you live (including under GDPR and CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information, to object to or restrict certain processing, and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@ather.com and we'll respond within 30 days.

08Security

We use industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical safeguards (encryption in transit, access controls, and least-privilege permissions) to protect your information. No method of transmission is perfectly secure, but we work hard to keep your data safe and to notify you promptly in the unlikely event of a breach.

09International transfers

We're based in the United States and may process information in countries with different data-protection laws. Where we transfer data internationally, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses to ensure your information remains protected.

10Children's privacy

Our site and services are intended for businesses and are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we'll delete it.

11Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our practices or the law evolve. When we do, we'll revise the "last updated" date above and, for material changes, notify you by email or a notice on our site. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.

12Contact us

Questions about this policy or your data? Email privacy@ather.com or write to Ather LLC, 2847 Mission Street, San Francisco, CA 94110.
